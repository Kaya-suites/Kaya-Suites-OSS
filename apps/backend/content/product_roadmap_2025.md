## Vision

Kaya Suites will become the AI-native knowledge operating system for fast-growing companies. Every document is live, cited, and kept current by AI — not by humans filing tickets.

## Q1 2025 Priorities

The primary Q1 focus is the enterprise analytics dashboard, which was originally planned for November 2024 but slipped to January due to a data-model migration. The dashboard will expose per-user search metrics, document health scores, and stale-reference counts to admins. Target launch: January 31, 2025.

Secondary Q1 work includes APAC market entry. A Singapore-region deployment will be live by March 15, 2025. Localization (Japanese UI strings) is scoped for Q2.

## Q2 2025 Priorities

Q2 is focused on the AI edit workflow improvements based on beta-user feedback. Key investments:

- **Bulk propose**: the agent will be able to propose edits across multiple documents in a single turn.
- **Approval queues**: editors can queue approved changes and commit them in batches.
- **Audit log**: every approved edit is recorded with user, timestamp, and before/after diff.

## Deferred Items

The following items were deprioritized from H1 2025 based on customer feedback:

- Ollama / local-model support (now targeted for v0.5, H2 2025)
- BYO-key support for enterprise (now Q3 2025)
- Slack integration (moved to Q3 2025 after sales reported low demand)

## Success Metrics

By the end of H1 2025 we target:

- 500 active organizations (up from 87 at end of Q3 2024)
- NPS ≥ 45
- Mean time from document creation to first agent citation < 5 minutes
- P99 search latency < 400ms
