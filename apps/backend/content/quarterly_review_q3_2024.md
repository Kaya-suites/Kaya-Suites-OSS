## Executive Summary

This document summarizes the financial and operational performance for the third quarter of 2024. The company delivered solid results across all key metrics, with revenue and customer retention both exceeding internal targets.

## Revenue

Revenue grew 12% year-over-year, driven primarily by the enterprise segment. Total revenue for Q3 2024 reached $22.4M, up from $20.0M in Q3 2023. The mid-market segment contributed 28% of total revenue, while SMB accounted for 14%.

## Customer Metrics

Customer retention improved from 87% to 91% in the same period. New customer acquisition increased by 8% quarter-over-quarter, with 142 new logos added. Average contract value (ACV) rose to $48k from $41k, reflecting upsell success in the enterprise tier.

## Headcount

The company ended the quarter with 312 employees, up from 287 at the start of Q3. Engineering grew by 14 headcount, primarily in the infrastructure and ML platform teams. Sales and marketing headcount remained flat.

## Operating Expenses

Total operating expenses were $18.1M for the quarter, yielding an operating margin of 19.2%. R&D spend was $6.4M (28.6% of revenue), in line with guidance. Cost of goods sold improved to 32% of revenue from 35% in Q3 2023.

## Outlook

Management expects continued growth in Q4, with revenue guidance of $24M–$26M. Key initiatives include the launch of the enterprise analytics dashboard (targeting November) and expansion into the APAC market (Q1 2025). Headcount additions of 20–25 are planned for Q4, focused on sales and customer success.
